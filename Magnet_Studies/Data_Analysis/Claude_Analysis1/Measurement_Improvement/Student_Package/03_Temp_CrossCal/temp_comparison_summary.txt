NOTE: Neither sensor directly measures the sample's internal temperature.
  - Arduino AM2302: measures ambient air (may be affected by drafts,
    laptop fans, HVAC airflow; +/-0.5 C spec accuracy)
  - Teslameter probe: in contact with sample surface (may include
    self-heating bias, but physically closer to sample temperature)
  - Which is the better proxy for the temperature correction is an
    OPEN QUESTION to be resolved by the summer cross-calibration study.
  - AM2302 advantage: synchronous with Helmholtz measurement time
  - Teslameter advantage: in thermal contact with the actual sample
  - Target: reduce temperature uncertainty to +/-0.3 C by understanding
    and modeling the offset, regardless of which sensor is primary.
