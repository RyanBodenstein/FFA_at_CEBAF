     9 of 30 Y-plates use Landauer InLight stated accuracy (10%)

     Rod-derived plates: N=21

     Independent calibration estimate: 5.7%
     Ratio (Kirsten / independent): mean=2.60  range=0.49–6.07

4. CONCLUSIONS
----------------------------------------

  - Rod-propagated dose uncertainties: 3%–35% (Kirsten's sigma)
  - OSL-matched plates: 10%% assigned (Landauer InLight stated accuracy)
  - Measurement-propagated calibration uncertainty: ~6% (from OD replicates)
  - Degradation baseline SEMs are small (~0.02–0.10%%)
  - Temperature correction is the dominant systematic for absolute values
  - The gain-immune NdFeB-SmCo differential eliminates the largest systematic
  - Rod calibration R²: 0.851–0.9999 (3 of 4 > 0.85)
  - Replicate OD spread: ~5–11%% (median), primary measurement noise source
  - None of the dose or degradation uncertainties are large enough to
    explain the observed NdFeB-SmCo differential of -0.208%% (7.7σ)
