6b. WHY Dy DAMAGE IS AMPLIFIED
------------------------------------------------------------------------
In NdFeB manufacturing, Dy is added via grain boundary diffusion (GBD).
This concentrates Dy preferentially at grain boundaries, where:
  (1) Dy(n,gamma) captures deposit energy DIRECTLY at boundaries
  (2) Grain boundary integrity controls intergranular coercivity
  (3) Even small boundary damage can reduce bulk Hci
  (4) Volume-averaged capture underestimates local boundary dose

Observed extra degradation: N42EH - N52SH = -0.082%%
This is consistent with Dy-driven grain boundary damage, since:
  - N42EH has 1-2%% Dy (concentrated at GBs), N52SH has ZERO
  - Slot randomization rules out positional artifacts
  - B content identical → B-10 pathway cannot explain the difference
  - SmCo grades have identical composition → SmCo difference is microstructural
